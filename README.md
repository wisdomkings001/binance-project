## What is BNex AI?

BNex AI is a smart, all-in-one Binance crypto intelligence app that gives users real-time market data and AI-powered trading analysis in one clean, easy-to-use interface. It was built to help both beginners and experienced traders make better, safer decisions in the crypto market — without needing to jump between multiple platforms or tools.

The app has two main sections and one powerful floating assistant, all working together seamlessly.

---

## 📊 Section 1 — Live Trading Dashboard

The Trading section displays real-time prices for over 60 of the most traded coins on Binance, pulled directly from the Binance API and automatically updated every 10 seconds.

For every coin, users can see the current price, 24-hour price change (with green and red indicators), the day's highest and lowest price, total trading volume, and number of trades. Users can search for any coin by name or symbol, and sort the list by volume, percentage change, or price. Tapping any coin opens a detailed breakdown panel showing all its stats at a glance.

Coins covered include all major assets — Bitcoin, Ethereum, BNB, Solana, XRP, Dogecoin, Cardano, Avalanche, Polygon, Chainlink, Shiba Inu, Pepe, Injective, Aptos, Arbitrum, Optimism, and over 40 more.

---

## 💬 Section 2 — Ask Questions

The second section is a dedicated AI chatbot that users can ask anything crypto-related. Whether someone wants to understand how DeFi works, learn about liquidity pools, get help with Binance features like Launchpad or Futures, understand tokenomics, learn about NFTs, or get clarity on blockchain concepts like Layer 2 and smart contracts — BNex AI answers it all in plain, easy-to-understand language.

This section is designed to educate and empower users at every level, from complete beginners to advanced traders looking for deeper insight.

---

## 🤖 Floating Analyzer Bot

The standout feature of BNex AI is the floating analyzer bot — a persistent AI assistant that sits at the bottom right of the screen and is accessible at all times.

What makes it unique is that it reads the live market data directly. When a user selects any coin from the Trading section and taps "Analyze with BNex AI", the bot automatically receives that coin's current price, 24-hour change, volume, highs and lows — and delivers a full trading analysis on the spot.

The bot looks at the market conditions and gives the user a safe, conservative trading recommendation. It always includes risk management advice such as suggested stop-loss levels, position sizing, and cautions about volatility. Users can also chat freely with the bot, asking follow-up questions or requesting a broader market overview across all coins.

The goal is simple: give every user access to the kind of smart, data-driven analysis that was previously only available to professional traders.
